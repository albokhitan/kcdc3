Containers, slices, and stories

Container model basics

Containers have one or more slices, and each slice contains one or more stories. We set the size, format, and color options at the slice level.

Medium Story Slices

Stories in medium (size-md) slices are one-third or one-quarter width on desktops. They share the same markup as large and extra-large stories. When a story links to an external site, the dateline contains the name of the outside site and gains an icon.

Large Story Slices

Stores in large (size-lg) slices are half-width on desktops. They share the same markup as medium and large stories. Large stories can also have backgrounds.

Extra-Large Story Slices

Stories in extra-large (size-xl) slices always fill the screen's width. They share the same markup as medium and large stories. There is an option for using an excerpt from the text in place of the image. Large stories can also have backgrounds.
